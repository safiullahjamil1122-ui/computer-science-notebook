# Code of Conduct

This project follows a simple standard: be respectful, be constructive, and assume good intent.

## Expected behavior

- Use professional, inclusive language.
- Critique ideas, not people.
- Keep feedback specific and actionable.
- Respect maintainers' time and the repository's documentation-first purpose.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Deliberate sabotage of documentation or review discussions.
- Repeated off-topic or hostile behavior.

## Enforcement

If a discussion becomes unproductive or harmful, maintainers may moderate comments, close issues, or remove contributions.
